Bridge #1

↓ Here is the scale model of our bridge #1

The first bridge we built was a truss bridge, it containes 5 sections with triangles facing outwards on both sides. Each sections is 5cm long in length. In total the bridge is 60cm long and the top is 50cm long, with a heigth of roughly 7cm and a width of 10cm. The total weight of our bridge is 150g. Our bridge held 4 pounds because the end of our bridge snaped which lead to us getting 12.1 points.
