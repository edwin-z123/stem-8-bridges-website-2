First bridge!

Design process

It was a very narrow bridge.

Measurments:

-Length: 60cm

-Height: 20cm

-Width: 5cm

Our thoughts were that if we had a thin bridge we would have leftover spagetti for extra support. For the next test we have lots of new ideas and strategies to make test #2 better than the last.

Problem

-Off balance / too tippy

-Not wide enough

Results

Sadly our bridge broke while we were finishing it up on the day of the crushing.

Reflections and improvement

We have definitely learnt that wide bridges are better than narrow.
Making sure the top is leveled
Most of all we will try to avoid getting disqualifyed
